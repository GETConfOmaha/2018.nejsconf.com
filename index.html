Gender Equity in Tech Conf, GET CONF for short, is a one-day tech conference by women, for everyone. To amplify and celebrate the voices of women and non-men, and educate and connect people of all genders.

What we mean when we say "gender equity"

You're probably familiar with the concept of equality, which basically means giving everyone the same (equal) access to resources and opportunity.

Equity on the other hand, involves a more holistic approach that takes into account factors like historical context, systemic biases, privilege and other things beyond individual control that may impact someone's ability to achieve success. Instead of assuming that given the same set of resources all people will be able to succeed, equity is about realizing that due to extenuating circumstances it's more appropriate to meet individuals where they are. Equity means giving people the resources they need to succeed as an individual, and accounts for the fact that for some in groups that have been historically disadvantaged it may take additional resources.

Another way to think about it:
It's not enough to have a seat at the dinner table if no one offers you a plate or food to actually partake in the meal.
